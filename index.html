About Me

My research focuses on developing computational methods and models to introduce voltage control in power flow analysis for DC grids. Currently, I aim to design and investigate power flow approaches to integrate voltage control taking into account also computational time and accuracy.

Keywords: power flow analysis; iterative methods; recursive methods; voltage control.
